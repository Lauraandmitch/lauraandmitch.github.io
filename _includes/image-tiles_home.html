The Day They Got Engaged

Every year for our anniversary we plan a hike. On our fourth anniversary we decided to celebrate the day with one of our favourite hikes at Square Rock in Canberra’s Namadgi National Park. We’ve always loved the views so we got up before dawn to make sure we could watch the sun rise on our day. At top we took the next step in our adventure together, we said yes!
